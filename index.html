Aaron Kwok

King of Stage

Aaron Kwok is a Hong Kong dancer, singer and actor. Known as the Canto-Pop "Four Heavenly King". Kwok's onstage dancing and displays is influenced by Michael Jackson. While most of his songs are in the dance-pop genre.

  • 1965- Born in Hong Kong
  • 1984- His talent for dancing, he joined as a dancer training course at the Hong Kong television station TVB.
  • 1987- Acting training course and became a TV actor, where played minor roles in dramas.
  • 1990- TV commercial in Taiwan for the Honda motorcycle DJ-1RR. Gained him instant popularity in Taiwan. Also, famous for his “center-split hair style”. Began his music career in mandopop albums including famous song Loving You Forever, which sold more than 1 million copies across Asia.
  • 1991- Returned to Hong Kong. Continued to advance his career in the Cantopop industry. Ranked as one of the “Four Heavenly Kings” and name as “King of Stage” or “Dancing King”. Had won his first Best New Artist Award then won a major award every year.
  • 1997-2000- Jade Solid Gold Top 10 Awards, The Most Popular Male Singer and Asia Pacific Most Popular Male Singer Award.
  • 1998- Collaborated with Janet Jackson for Pepsi Chinese version of Ask for More.
  • 2001-present – Hold over 200 sold-out World Tours Live in Concerts.
  • 2005- 42nd Golden Horse Awards and Golden Deer Award as the Best Leading Actor for the role in the film Divergence.
  • 2006- 43rd Gold Horse Awards as the Best Leading Actor for the role in the film After This Our Exile.
  • 2008- "Aaron Kwok De Show Reel Extension Live" concert at the Hong Kong Asia World Arena with the largest revolving stage measured at 10m x 9.44m 450-degree and created a new entry for the Guinness Book of World Records.
  • 2013- Asian Artist of the Year in China at the Mnet Asian Music Awards. 5th China Image Film Festival Best Actor in the film Silent Witness.
  • 2014- Best Sales Local Male Artistes, Vocalist, and Releases Local Live Recordings at the IFPI Hong Kong Top Sales Music Award
  • 2015- 14th New York Asian Film Festival Star Asia Award in the film Port of Call.
  • 2016- 35th Hong Kong Film Awards for the Best Actor for his role in the film Port of Call.
  • 2017- Launched of his own brand [AaKode+] Male Skin Care Products and [AKFS+] Hair Care Products.

    • Hobbies

  • 2008- Ford Formula Campus Championship
  • 2009-2011- Won the Championship of Clio Cup China Series.
  • 2012-13- Audi R8 LMS Cup.
  • 2016- Ferrari Racing Days, FIA Formula E Touring Car Challenge by FRD Motosports
  • 2016- FRD LMP3 Series
